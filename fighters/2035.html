Vitor Henrique Oliveira

Vitor Henrique Oliveira, also known as “Vitinho” is a jiu jitsu black belt and a representative of the well respected GF Team academy (formerly of Pit-bull Jiu Jitsu). Vitor Henrique made himself noticed to the mainstream grappling community through his aggressive style of competing, a style that he used in of the best matches of the 2012 world championships (against Bill Cooper). Oliveira has also placed in important competitions such as the IBJJF World Championship, World Cup (CBJJO), the Brasileiro ( IBJJF Brazilian nationals), American nationals and Rio International Open.

Vitor Henrique Biography

Vitor Oliveira was born on the 28 of April, 1986 in Teresópolis, state of Rio de Janeiro–Brazil. Vitor started training jiu-jitsu when he was 8 years old at the famous Pitbull academy, influenced by his father who already practiced the martial art.

Adilson de Lima, also known as professor “Bitta” was Vitor Henrique’s first coach and the man that graduated Vitor in all his jiu-jitsu belts, including his black belt which happened when Vitor was 22 years old. Since receiving his black belt, Henrique became a team member for GFTeam.
